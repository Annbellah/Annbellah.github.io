My team and I developed a model focused on minimizing investment risks and optimizing asset allocation for Apple Inc. stock. The primary objective of the project was to provide accurate, data-driven insights to improve portfolio management and support better financial decision-making. By analyzing historical stock data, we created a forecasting tool that helps investors make informed decisions, reducing uncertainty and risk in their investment strategies.
